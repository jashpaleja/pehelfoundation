Pehel Foundation

Pehel Foundation started in December 2019. Our founder, Ms. Riya Thakkar had only one aim in mind when she started Pehel. The idea was to help the neglected sections of the society in any way we can. After 20+ interns, multiple events and incredible support from our sponsors and donors, Pehel has come a long way. But there is still a long way to go before we can say we made a significant difference. We know our small initiatives may not change the world, but we are ready to do our best if we can impact even one life or make one kid smile. Aakhir kisiko to pehel karni hogi!
